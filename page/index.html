                                                                 My Home Town Akole

Akole is a taluka (sub-district) in the Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra, India. Here are some key facts about Akole taluka. Akole is a city and taluka in the Akole tehsil of Ahmednagar District, Maharashtra, India. It is situated within the Sahyadri Mountains of the Western Ghats. The city has several historical sites that reflect its connection to the history and culture of Maharashtra
