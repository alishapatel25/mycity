About Akole

Akole is a taluka (sub-district) in the Ahmednagar district of Maharashtra, India. Here are some key facts about Akole taluka

Location

Akole taluka is situated in the western part of Ahmednagar district, approximately 120 km southeast of Mumbai. The taluka is nestled in the Sahyadri mountain range, with the Akole hill station being a popular tourist destination. The region is known for its scenic beauty, with lush forests, waterfalls, and valleys.

Places of interest

Places of interest are Some popular tourist attractions in Akole taluka are Akole hill station , Bhandardara Dam , Randha Falls , Kalsubai peak (the highest peak in Maharashtra) , Shri Shankar Temple
